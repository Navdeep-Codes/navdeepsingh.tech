ABOUT ME

The person behind the pixels

Hi, I'm Navdeep

I'm a digital artist and developer specializing in creating pixel art with anime aesthetics.

My journey began with a fascination for Japanese animation and pixel art styles, which I've combined into my own unique visual language. I believe in the power of visual storytelling and creating experiences that resonate on both emotional and aesthetic levels.

I've collaborated with animation studios, indie developers, and brands looking to stand out with distinctive visual styles. My approach blends traditional pixel techniques with modern design principles to create work that feels both nostalgic and contemporary.
